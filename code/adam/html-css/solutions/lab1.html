Introduction

Kobe Bean Bryant was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, U.S. and was an American proffesional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers. He helped the Lakers win five NBA championships in his 20 year tenure there. He played at Lower Merion High school in Ardmore, Pennsylvania. He sadly passed away on January 26th, 2020 in Calabasas, California in a helicopter accident along with his daughter Gigi.
