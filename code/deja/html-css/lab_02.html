About Us

We are an all inclusive education center for young adults between the ages 17-25. Our main purpose is to teach the life skills necessary to establish a solid foundation for success as an adult.

Courses We Offer:

  2. Understanding Your Taxes
  3. Meal Prep
  4. Career Planning
