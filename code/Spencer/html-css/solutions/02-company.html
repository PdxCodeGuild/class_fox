Hello, My name is Spencer and I’m a metalsmith, based in Portland, OR. I’m the designer and maker of one of a kind, handmade wearable works of art. Since 2012 I have been studying, learning and refining my knowledge of metal arts. My work is driven by the process of creating. From a young child I have been drawn to unique, and simple organic designs which are an integral part of the pieces I create. My pieces are almost entirely one of a kind and they evolve, change and grow depending how I am feeling and the inspiration that comes to me
